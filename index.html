Ray Martin is a lead vocalist and bass player based in the Nashville area, bringing a vocal style that draws deep from the well of Gregg Allman, Warren Haynes, and Chris Stapleton — that place where rock, blues, country, and a touch of jazz all bleed into each other.

Whether holding down the low end or stepping to the mic, Ray brings feel first. Strong with background vocals, at home on any stage.
